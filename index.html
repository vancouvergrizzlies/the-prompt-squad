About

Meet Cole Penner

I started The Prompt Squad because I saw the same thing happening over and over — business owners and professionals in the Phoenix area hearing about AI, knowing they should be using it, but having no idea where to start. And the options out there? Either figure it out yourself with YouTube videos, or pay some consulting firm $10,000 for a strategy deck.

I thought there should be something in between. Someone who actually shows up, sits down with you, sets it up on your computer, and makes sure you know how to use it before they leave. That's what we do.

I work with AI every single day. I've spent hundreds of hours learning what works, what doesn't, and how to make these tools actually useful for real work — not just tech demos. I bring that experience directly to your business so you don't have to go through the same learning curve.

Based right here in Gilbert, Arizona. If you're in the Phoenix metro area, I'd love to help you get started.
